Why do we do it?

Lunchtime Labs is a small-but-mighty boutique software development shop based in NYC.

We are inspired by elegant design in technology--by the idea that, regardless of how technically sophisticated an app is underneath, it should be effortless to use.

In an age of clickbait, internet addiction and compulsive phone use, we aim to build apps that set people free and bring them closer to their souls.

In an age where more generations of people find themselves estranged and confused by technology, we aim to build apps that are simple and inclusive.

Our specialists have years of experience developing state-of-the-art native, web, mobile and desktop apps. We love taking projects from the ideation stage through product design, user testing, into launch and beyond.
