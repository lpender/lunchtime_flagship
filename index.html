Why do we do it?

Lunchtime Labs is a bespoke software development shop based in NYC.

We are inspired by elegant design in technology--by the idea that, regardless of how technically sophisticated an app is underneath, it should be simple to use.

Our team has years of experience designing and developing state-of-the-art native, web, mobile and desktop apps. We love taking projects from the ideation stage through product design, user testing, into launch and beyond.

How do we do it?

We adhere to the principles of product design set forth by Google, thoughtbot, and other thought-leaders in the field. Following these concepts, We employ a design driven process to validate mockups via user testing on prototypes before "building deep".

Once a product idea has been validated, our team of experienced engineers will work closely with you to build a production-ready app, website, or other product and to launch it to a wider audience.

We offer ongoing advice, guidance, and software development to a variety of digital businesses in many areas, including:

  • Product Design
  • User Experience Design
  • Graphic Design
  • User Testing and Business Validation
  • Web/iOs/Android app development
  • Digital Marketing Strategization
